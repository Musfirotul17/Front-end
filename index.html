The Game Awards 2023: Biggest Announcements, Trailers, & New Game Reveals

The Game Awards 2023 isn't just an opportunity to celebrate the best games of the year; it also gives developers the chance to announce what they're working on next. In between presentations, speeches, and performances, each year's TGA show takes the opportunity to air trailers for every manner of video game release. Brand-new reveals, long-awaited updates, DLC announcements, hardware, merch, accessories - these are as much a part of the show as the awards themselves, and remain a big part of why many keep watching.

The Game Awards 2023 did away with the now-iconic "World Premiere" title card for some of its trailers, previously used to denote when a trailer was airing for the first time that night. However, that doesn't mean there were any fewer big reveals this year. As always, TGA 2023 was full of welcome updates, intriguing reveals, and massive surprises. Here's everything there is to know.
